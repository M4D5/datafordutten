Don't rely on this page for the real date of your exam. The purpose here is to give you a general idea of when and how long your exam period is.

The exam dates for a course are found using the following sources, ordered by priority

  • Explicitly stated exam dates for the course (Mat 1, for example)
  • The exam date for the course's "Exam Placement"
  • The exam date for the course's "Schedule Placement"

What do the scores mean?

Average Grade

This one is easy. It's just the average grade from the course.

Average Grade Percentile

All courses are ranked against each other based on average grade.

Percent passed

The percentage of people who attended the exam that passed.

Course Rating Percentile

All course are ranked based on the question "Overall I think the course is good" from course reviews.

Workscore Percentile

Ranked based on the question "5 points are allocated to 9h./week (45 h./week in the 3-week period). I think my workload in the course is [Much less..Much more]"
